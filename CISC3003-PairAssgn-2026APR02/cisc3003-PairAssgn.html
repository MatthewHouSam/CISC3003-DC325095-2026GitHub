Our Studio

Focused on learning and real-world practice

Our business site is based on the flexbox responsive layout practice suggested in the CISC3003 Pair Assignment. We follow a mobile-first design, then progressively enhance the layout for tablet and desktop viewports, ensuring a smooth experience for visitors.

  • One-page style for each business subpage (Profile, Summary, Showcase, Qualification).
  • Semantic HTML structure aligned with course guidelines.
  • Consistent navigation, branding, and footer across all pages.
