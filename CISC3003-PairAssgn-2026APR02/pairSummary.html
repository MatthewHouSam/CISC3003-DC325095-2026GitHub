Business Summary

Our goals, commitments and future directions.

This page summarises what Lindsay & Matthew Web Studio aims to achieve through this pair assignment: demonstrating our competencies in layout, responsiveness, and portfolio planning, while aligning with the official CISC3003 assessment rubric.

Our Business Goals

What This Studio Wants to Achieve

Concrete goals connected to the requirements of the pair assignment and our own learning.

Goal 01

Deliver a complete multi-page business site

We aim to implement all required business pages (Home, Profile, Summary, Showcase, Qualification) using a consistent one-page style for each subpage, similar to the examples from Suggested Exercise 03.

Goal 02

Integrate portfolio and project demos

The business site should link clearly to both partners' portfolio sites and three project demos based on existing multi-page examples from CISC3003, such as landing pages and e-commerce demos.

Goal 03

Follow mobile-first and validation requirements

Our layouts should adapt smoothly across mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports, and pass HTML validation after deployment.

Professional Commitments

How We Work as a Studio

Agreements between partners that support quality and reliability.

To Our Clients

Clear communication & realistic scope

Even though this is a course assignment, we imagine our audience as real clients. We commit to clear navigation, clear labelling of pages, and realistic descriptions of what we can deliver with HTML, CSS and simple JavaScript.

  • Explain what each page and section is for.
  • Use concise copywriting with headings and subheadings.
  • Keep design choices consistent with the rest of the site.
To Each Other

Shared responsibility and recognition

We commit to recognising each partner's effort fairly. The footer on every business page clearly shows both student IDs and names, matching the assessment rubric requirement.

  • Both portfolios are reachable from the business site.
  • Project demos are drawn fairly from our existing work.
  • Tasks are divided so that both partners can contribute.

Future Directions

How We Plan to Extend This Work

Ideas for turning this pair assignment into a foundation for future projects.

Direction 01

Convert into a real freelance site

After CISC3003, we could update text content and images to match real freelance projects, but keep the same page structure and responsive layout built in this assignment.

Direction 02

Add more project demos

New projects from future courses (e.g., JavaScript frameworks, back-end services) can be added to the Showcase and project demo pages.

Direction 03

Improve accessibility and performance

We can later add ARIA attributes, keyboard navigation improvements, and further optimise images and CSS for faster loading times.
